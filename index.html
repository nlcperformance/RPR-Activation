RPR DIAGNOSTIC MAP

CLINICAL NEURO-LYMPHATIC ACTIVATION TARGETS

ANTERIOR CHAIN (FRONT)

GLUTES (Base of Skull/Jaw)
CUFF (Under Clavicle)
DIAPHRAGM (Sternum)
PSOAS (Below/Lateral to Navel)

1. Glutes (Base of Skull/Jaw)

Scrub deeply directly under the jawline/base of the ear. Flips the primary hip extension switch.

2. Diaphragm (Sternum)

Rub vigorously up and down the center of the breastbone. Crucial for core bracing and breathing.

3. Psoas (Lower Abdomen)

Find the navel. Move 1 inch down, 1 inch out. Dig deep into the tissue. Releases tight hips.

POSTERIOR CHAIN (BACK)

QL & LAT SLING (12th Rib)
HAMSTRINGS (Sacrum/Tailbone)

4. QL & Lateral Sling (12th Rib)

Scrub the bottom edge of the lowest floating rib on the back. Unbinds the opposite lat and shoulder.

5. Hamstrings (Sacrum)

Vigorously scrub the flat bone at the base of the spine (tailbone). Prevents hamstring pulls.
